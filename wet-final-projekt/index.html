The Game

The reason why I chose this game as the topic of my final project is, that this game is very dear to my heart. I used to play it way back in elementary school, where I racked up over 2.5 thousand hours. Then, wanting to do other things in life, so I took a break from it. Over the course of 2018's winter break I however redownloaded the game and have found it's charm once again.

I mainly play support classes such as the medic or the engineer, because I really enjoy the teamwork in this game. There are many different ways in which you can play each class and that opens up tons of possibilities, as there are loads of synergies between the different class playstyles.
